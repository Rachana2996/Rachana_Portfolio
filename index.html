03 — Consumer Product
Rapid, Same-Day & Next-Day Delivery
From standard to instant commerce · Repro India
+
Problem
Cart abandonment data flagged delivery speed as a key drop-off. Customer complaints confirmed it. Competitors had faster options. A gifting segment opportunity sat unreachable without fast fulfilment.
My approach
  • Discovery: user interviews, cart data, competitive benchmarking
  • Defined 3 tiers: 2-hour rapid, same-day, next-day
  • Scoped 3 integrations: courier API, WMS, pin-code engine
  • Defined slot logic, cut-off times, fallback rules per tier
Outcome
All 3 tiers live in production. Platform now competes on delivery speed. Rapid delivery unlocked the gifting segment. Pin-code serviceability engine cut failed deliveries and complaints significantly.

04 — Civic Tech · Personal Project
ClearAir — Ward-level GRAP Enforcement Platform
Concept design · Delhi air quality accountability gap
+
Problem
Delhi's Green Delhi App had thousands of overdue citizen complaints with zero public enforcement accountability. GRAP restrictions were announced but compliance was untracked at ward level. Citizens had no way to see whether their complaints were acted on — or by whom.
My approach
  • Mapped the enforcement gap: complaint submission existed, enforcement visibility did not
  • Developed 3 core personas — resident reporter, ward officer, policy observer
  • Scoped MVP: ward-level GRAP compliance dashboard + citizen complaint tracker with public status visibility
  • Mapped stakeholders across MCD, DPCC, RWAs, and civic journalists
  • Defined GTM targeting RWA networks and civic media as initial distribution
Key insight
The existing app solved complaint submission — ClearAir solves complaint accountability. The differentiator is a public enforcement layer: every ward officer action (or inaction) is visible to citizens in real time. Transparency as the enforcement mechanism.
